Account Deletion Request

How to Delete Your Account

If you would like to delete your PEMDAS Challenge account and all associated data, please follow the steps below:

  1. Send an email to your@email.com
  2. Use subject: Delete My Account
  3. Include your user ID or username

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • User account information
  • Game progress and scores
  • Leaderboard data
  • Coins and achievements

Data Retention

Your data will be permanently deleted within 7 days of request.
